Game Instructions

Welcome to Ore Miner! Your goal is to become the ultimate miner by collecting as many ores as possible. Here's how to play:

  1. Click the ore to mine and collect ores.
  2. Each ore click earns you points, and you can increase your mining efficiency with upgrades.
  3. Use the "Multiplier" upgrade to increase your ore collection rate.
  4. Unlock "Auto-Clickers" to automatically mine ores for you.
  5. Activate the "Bonus" for a temporary 200% ore collection boost.

Keep an eye on your score at the top of the screen and strategize to become the richest miner in the game. Have fun and happy mining!
